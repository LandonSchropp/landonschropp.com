Biophilia and Our Environment

A talk by Scott Wyatt I attended at TEDxSeattle

The design of the environments we inhabit affect how we think.

Biophilia is the bond between human beings and other living systems. If there's a tree within 100 yards of your window, your stress goes down and your cognitive performance goes up.

If you want to have a great meeting, go outside.
